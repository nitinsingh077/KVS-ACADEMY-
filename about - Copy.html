Our Story

How we became the most fun place to learn

From a Small Classroom to a Wonderland of Learning

KVS Academy started in 2005 with just one classroom and a dream to make education fun and engaging for every child. Our founder, Professor Kartik Sharma, believed that learning should never be boring.

What began as a small tutoring center has now grown into a full-fledged academy with over 500 students and 50 dedicated teachers. We've maintained our core philosophy: education should spark joy and curiosity.

Throughout our journey, we've developed innovative teaching methods that combine traditional learning with interactive activities, games, and creative projects. Our cartoon-inspired campus creates an environment where imagination thrives.
